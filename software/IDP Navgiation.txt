Junction types:
* Left-right                                        L-R        
* Forward-left                                        F-L        
* Forward-right                                        F-R        
* End-block (no sensors on)                        E-B
* End-outpost (all sensors on)                        E-O


Start to residential 1:
* Blind forwards into line follow
* Left turn at L-R
* Right turn at F-R
* Pick-up at E-B


Residential 1 to green outpost:
* 180 turn
* Right turn at L-R
* Left turn at L-R
* Drop off at E-O


Residential 1 to red outpost:
* 180 turn
* Left turn at L-R
* Forward at F-R
* Right at L-R
* Drop off at E-O


Green outpost to residential 2:
* 180 turn
* Forward at F-R
* Right at F-R
* Forward at F-L
* Right at F-R
* Pick up at E-B


Red outpost to residential 2:
* 180 turn
* Forward at F-L
* Left at F-L
* Left at F-L
* Pick up at E-B


Residential 2 to green outpost:
* 180 turn
* Left at L-R
* Forward at F-R
* Left at L-R
* Forward at F-L
* Drop off at E-O


Residential 2 to red outpost:
* 180 turn
* Right at L-R
* Right at L-R
* Forward at F-R
* Drop off at E-O


Green outpost to finish:
* 180 turn
* Right at F-R
* Forward at F-L
* Right at F-R
* Straight on blind at L-R until detects line at back of box


Red outpost to finish:
* 180 turn
* Left at F-L
* Left at F-L
* Straight on blind at L-R until detects line at back of box
